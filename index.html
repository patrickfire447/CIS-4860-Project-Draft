📋1. Project Title & Description

Project Title: Skill Acquisition of Steam Services

My topic is a report about Skill Acquisition of Steam Services, and it's about how steam employees develop their skills within the workplace with different applications with the goal of creating content for customers. My report will be beneficial because it will identify which steam programs and services most effectively build in-demand skills. Highlighting emerging skill gaps in the job market and how different steam initiatives prepare others for specific industries.

  • Types of databases used for data (login data or other types)

  • Find upcoming updates or changes to steam within 6 months or less

❌ Out of Scope
